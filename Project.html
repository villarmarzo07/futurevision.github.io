License plate detection algorithm

Project description

In FutureVision we want to focus on tackling and solving traffic based problems from new points of view, combining classical computer vision algorithms with innovatives technologies such us convolutional neural networks.

At this moment, the main project we are working on is the development of a fast and efficient vehicle and license plate detection algorithm. License plate detection is still a very requested topic, with a great variety of practical applications that improves our every day life and security on road. 

Our algorithm will be capable of locating the cars and license plates in the camera image, recognizing their characters as well. This information can be used for several purposes, some of them will be explained in the next secion.

Altought this topic has already some proposed solutions, at Future vision we are investigating an hybrid AI-CV approach that we expect to overcome the current state of the art methodologies

Algorithm applications

1. Road applications

Thanks  to our efficient algorithm, license plate detection in highways and interurban roads will be no longer a problem. Our solution combined with cameras located in high positions will allow local governments to guarantee the safety of the drivers.

Using a faster algorithm will assure that the infractor's license plate is detected correctly, assuring that the correspondent driver is fined. It is important to remark that the image capture conditions are really important, so the camera should be well calibrated.

2. Parking applications

Nowadays, most parking lots make use of a system based on computer vision and artificial intelligence (AI) to keep the car recorded. These systems detect automatically every vehicle's license plate to store that the car is in the parking lot. 

This kind of parkings are much more comfortable that the classical ones, in which you had to catch a ticket at the entrance and show it when you left. Now, this last step is not necessary neither in public nor private parkings, the system knows who is inside and, in case of private parkings, if a car can enter or not.

Therefore, we devote entirely  to offering an immersive and customized service, no matter the size of the parking. We focus on the idea of making drivers experience better and more comfortable, and this can be achieved by using our algorithm for car identification.
